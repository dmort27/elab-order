yaam zoo le nuav mas kuv loj sim paab txaaj muag. Peb yuav tsum paub
hab zeem tseeb tas lub title ntawd muaj nuj nqes npaum twg hab requires
qualification npaum twg. Yog yug tsi tau txawj txaus hab qualified yuav
tsum tsi txhob laam moog muab lug ntoo, nwg yuav ua siv ua npug rov rua
yug tug kheej.
