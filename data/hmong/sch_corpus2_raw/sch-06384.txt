"dlej" xwb.

ces ntxiv tug tsaj ntawv "l" rua kuas has tau meej ua "ntlog toj" mas
txhaj raug. Tawm sis Xeev Nruag Xyooj puab tsim hab khu peb cov ntawv
Moobleeg ces puab txawm muab sau ua "ndlog" lawm. Qhov nuav kuv pum tas
ncaim dleb zog lawm.
