ho los ua mong, Mhong. Ntshai yog Hmoob tsis muaj dab tsi ua lawm es

yawg Vajtswv (cov txiv plig) tsim los tseem tsis zoo Hmoob siab thiab.

ob cov ntawv los ntawm ib lub teb chaws li Hmoob. Kuv pom America cov
ntawv children's book, tseem muab pes ua ib qhov ntawv Hmoob Dawb, ib
