Cov phoojywg,
Peb tsis tau hnov ob tog lus, ces peb tsis txhob rawm ntseeg tg twg.
Raws li kuv xav, tus poj niam no cov lus yeej tseem ntseeg tsis tau
vim tias:
1. Yog muaj ib tug neeg hais rau koj hais tias "koj xav tuag hauv tsev
rau me nyuam pom los yuav mus tuag rau sab nraud kom me nyuam thxob
pom?", koj yeej tsis kam nrog nws caij tsheb mus qhov twg li lawm.
Tabsis tus poj niam no ho tseem nrog tus txiv caij tsheb mus thiab.
2. Yog tus txiv neej no yuav xav tuag tus poj niam xwb tiag tiag, ua
li cas tseem coj tsheb mus nrau choj? Yog ua li no ces tus yuav xub
tuag tshaj plaws yeej yog tus tsav tsheb (tus txiv) xwb nad!
3. Tus poj niam siab nyoos, yog yam nws tsis tau lawm ces nws yuav
tsis pub leej twg tau thiab. Thaum muaj deb hlob zoo dov cav, ces dov
cav xwb tiag.
4. Qhov reason uas tus txiv xav tua nws tus qub poj niam yog dab tsi?
Nkawv tab tom sib nrauj lawm, tus txiv yog tub ceev xwm (educated
man), hos tus txiv tab tom yov mus tos niam tshiab los, tus txiv puas
yuav lam ua tej yam ruam ruam li no kom nkag nkuaj mas?
Kuv kev xav xwb, yog muaj tseeb hais tias tus txiv no tried tua nws
tus qub poj niam no by coj tsheb mus tsoo choj li no mas tus txiv no
ruam tshaj tus npua lawm.
