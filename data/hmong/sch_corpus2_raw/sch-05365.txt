Zoo sab,
Hmoob tub txawj pheev kawm kawg txuj ci nyob MemKuj caag teb muaj nqes.
Tsim nyog Hmoob txhua tug zoo lub sab.  Zha Blong Xiong, kuv paab zoo
sab nrug koj txug koj teg txuj nuav, kws kawm txheeb txhua hmoog sab
heev.
Vaam tas teg txuj ci nuav koj kawm taag yuav luag paab tau Hmoob noob
neej kom pum txuj kev tsaav ntuj.  Kom hmoob tub pum deb ntxhais hluas
pum daav, sab luj, sab zoo, zoo lij xeeb, kom muaj nto npe.   Kom Hmoob
muaj neej nruag maab suav ntsaw coj.
Kawg nuav, kom tsim nyog txuj kev kawm txuj koj muaj, nyob yug lug ib
zag ntawm haiv Hmoob.  Tsim nyog tej kev koj khwv ab khwv daw.
