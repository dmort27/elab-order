motion to approve prior to the submission for standardization with
appropriate authority entity such as council of foreign language,
council of teaching and state department of education...

convert lus Hmoob ua lus Askiv and rovrais (vise versa). Tabsis kuv ho
tsis xav hais tias tsim nyog ntxhais lub Hmoob Dawb ua lus Hmoob Lees,
ntxhais lus Hmoob Dawb ua lus Hmoob Suav, lossis rovrais.

yog lus Hmoob xwb thiab twb sib thooj ua luaj es tseem yuav txhais li
cas ntxiv. Yog yuav txhais mas tsuas ntxhais tej lwb lo lus Hmoob rov
Khib = rab khib, tsaab kabcuam, rab uas coj los nog taws kom zoo ris,
tsaab kws coj lug nog tawg kuas zoo aub etc....
Kabcuam = rab khib, tsaab kabcuam, rab uas coj los nog taws kom zoo

Yus yog ib tug Hmoob lawm, yus yuav tsum paub cov lus hmoob uas yus
niaj hnub siv kom ntau npaum li ntau tau xwb. Txawm yus paub tsis tag
los mam kawm yeej tsis lig.
Hos hais txog ntawm cov Hmoob Suav uas lawv hu lawv tus kheej ua Hmoob
thiab hais lus thooj li peb, cov no tsis thas ua converter program rau
lawv. Peb cov uas nyob tebchaws vam meej no yuav tsum coj tus ntawv
RPA uas peb niaj nub siv no coj mus qhia thiab txhawb lawv, cheem
tamsim no tus ntawv "Hmongb" tseem tsis tau popular. Vim tus ntawv
Lastees no yog tus uas peb twb siv thoob plaws qab ntuj lawm (China,
Laos, Thialand, Vietnam, France, Canada, French Guyana etc...) Hos cov
uas lawv hu lawv tus kheej ua Hmub thiab Ab-Hmaub mas tsim cov
converter program los txhais lawv cov lus rau peb cov lus yeej yog ib
qho tsim nyog lawm.
