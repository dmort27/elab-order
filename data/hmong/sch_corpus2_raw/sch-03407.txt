tubtxib, rawsli kuv xav mas lolus lusxaiv no mas nws yog ib lo uas
muab two syllables los sibkoom ua ib los thiab nws yog (noun.)  For
example:  Nws nyiam hais lusxaiv.
tshaj xaiv - yog hais kom meej tiag ntshe yog tshaj lusxaiv tabsis
hais li no xwb los yeej totaub lawm. Qhovtseeeb seb nws sablus hais
mus seemtwg xwb.  Tshaj xaiv mas muaj ob lolus nyob rau rau hauv.
Tshaj yog verb txhias tias qhia lossis hais tawm kom tibneeg paub.
Xaiv yog noun - ib lo lossis cov lus uas tsis muaj tseeb li tau hais
dhau lawm.  Tagnrho tshaj xaiv - qhia tias muab cov lusxaiv uas tsis
yog tseeg ntawd qhia rau lwmtus paub.  Yog yuav hais li koj tau sau
tseg rau saum no mas ntshe yuav tau hais li no.  Xaiv tshaj xaiv -
tseem xaiv heevdua li yus ibtxwm xaiv los lawm.
taug xaiv - nyob ntawm soblus hais saib nws lub ntsiab mus rau
seemtwg.  kuv mus nrog lawv taug xaiv, no mas, yog kuv mus nrog lawv
soj tej lus xaiv lus ncua seb meejpem licas.  nws taug xaiv qhia kuv,
mas, yog nyiag qhia tabsis xyov puas muaj tseeb li nws hais lossis yus
hnov ntawd.
Txhob hais ntauntau tsam ho feeb tsis meej lawm.
