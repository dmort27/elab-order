Tug hluas, thawj tuam tswj koom hum, has tas txhob yuav nam yau lawm.
Tug laug has tas ua le ntawd mas tsi khov kag le.
Ob tug hluas, ib tug laug tuaj sis twg ua tuam thawj tswj koom hum,
puab peb leeg has peb zaaj lug saib puab yuav tuav lub koom hum le
caag.  Ob tug hluas has lug tsi nldaug zoo pes tsawg.  Ob tug cov ntsab
lug muaj kuab zoo.  Tug laug has lug nldaug dlua. Nwg cov ntsab lug
muaj kuab tsi npau ob tug hluas le.  Thaus xaiv tug laug tau lawm.
1.  Cov pej xeem xaiv le caag, cov zaaj lug puab has los yog puab cov
noob nyoog?
2.  Puab twb yeej xaiv tom tsev tuaj lawm los yog puab xaiv tom qaab
puab noog peb zaaj lug kws ob tug hluas hab ib tug laug tuaj moog sis
tw ntawd?

Peb cov neeg tawv dlaaj mas loj sim pauslitiv heev. Has txug tej nyuas
kev xaiv tsaa ua tuam tswj koomhum mivx le nuav xwb los peb yeej tsis
noog qaab noog hauv hab yeej tsis saib lub homphaj ntawm lub koomhum
hlo le. Peb tsuas saib dlej ua ntu, saib neeg ua phlu xwb, taab txawm
tug candidate twg yuav has raug hab tsis raug los xij peem. Tsi taag le
ntawd xwb, peb mas loj sim ua phav phuaj luj tshaaj plawg. Yog le mas
leej twg tau ces txhaj pheej muaj ntsis corrupt yaam tsis ntshai txuj
cai.
