Mej puag paub has tas moob ib tug xub xub cov nov cov ua yog txaj xaab
muas, kuv nov mas nwg tsaug tua thauam nwg tsov qaab tom luv chaw pob
baav ua phubthane lug nuav yog le caag tas, puab has tas thaum ntawd
nwg txeeb phubthane nyob nplog teb ntsug tsua maum suav lijfoom, ib cos
has tas yog peb cov moob vaaj tua,ib cov has tas yog cov kwvtij moob
lis tua,nuav yog lecaag tas.

Ib cov has tsua kuv has tas ib qhov kws nwg tsau tua yog vim nwg yuav
lwg tug tug luaj nkauj lwm, tabsim tug luag nkauj ntawd yog ib tug pum
ntsuag, yog vim lecaag tas.
