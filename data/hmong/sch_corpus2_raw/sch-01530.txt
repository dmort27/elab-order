Thaus pev nyav tuaj txug tebchaws nuav, cov laug sawv lug ua "nom qaug
cawv."  Ntev tuaj puab sawv lug ua "nom noj nyaj."  Uantej le GVP
ncaim, suavdlawg sawv lug ua "nom poob nyaj."
Yuav xaiv tug tshab nuav yog xaiv lug ua tug "nom dlaabtsi?"  Nom qaug
cawv los ua lug; nom noj nyaj los pum lug; hab nom poob nyaj los muaj
lawm; tej ntawd tsi muaj ib qho kws yuav ua rua Moob ca-sab.
Yuav xaiv ces xaiv tug kws muaj lub "tswm yim - wisdom, txujci -
knowlege, hab ncaajnceeg - ethically sound" kws Moob ca-sab.
