Kuv xav teb cov lus no kom ntau thiab meej ua nqe zuj zus tab sis xwm
li muaj lwm yam zoo tham dua lawm ces thiaj tsis tau teb. Tsam no twb
txais lawm, tab sis kuv xav tias tej lus peb sib hais (cav) txog Hmoob
ntsuab thiab Hmoob dawb no mas yuav rog peb Hmoob nyob tag mus li. Yam
nkaus lub qub roob hluav taws (old volcano) xwb ntag. Yog tias peb
tsis muab hais kom sib to taub thiab sib pab cob qhia peb tej me tub
me nyuam kom lawv paub qhov tseeb thiab meej.
Qhov tseeb tshaj ces kom peb yuav tau tsum los xyuas ntawm peb Hmoob
kev sib zeem kev txheeb kwv txheev tij. Qhov peb ntaus qi loj tshaj
tias, ib pab kwv tij, ib pawg kwv tij. Yog ua li cas peb thiaj hu tias
lawv yog ib tsev neeg, ib pab kwv tij koom tug dab qhuas. Thaum no peb
yuav tsis pom tias nej yog Hmoob ntsuab peb yog Hmoob dawb lawm.
Ob yam xwb uas kuv yuav muab ua pov thawj rau nej (Two and the only
crucial tests). Hmoob hais tais kev txheeb zes ces yog thaum koj tuag
tau hauv kuv tsev kuv tuag tau hauv koj tsev. Yog tias peb ib tug tuag
tau hauv ib tug tsev ces peb thiaj yog ib tsev neeg tiag tiag.
Yog li Hmoob ntsuab thiab Hmoob dawb yeej yog ib tsev neeg xwb. Vim
peb cov laus thaum ub, muaj qho pab mas Hmoob ntsuab tuag tau hauv
Hmoob dawb tsev. Hmoob dawb tuag tau hauv Hmoob ntsuab tsev, yog tias
lawv yog tib lub xeem lawm. Tab sis peb cov txawv xeem lawm yuav mus
tuag rau lwm tus tsis yog peb lub xeem lub tsev tsis tau li. Qhov no,
kuv niam qhia kuv tias kuv niam naim phog lawv cuab kwv tij muaj ob
pab li hais no. Muaj ib pab yeej yog hnav tsoos tsho Hmoob ntsuab hais
lus Hmoob ntsuab, hos ib pab yeej yog nag tsoos tsho Hmoob dawb hais
lus Hmoob dawb xwb, tab sis kev cai dab qhuas yog tib tug.
Qhov thib ob tuaj, peb Hmoob tsis hais ntsuab los dawb, yog peb yog
tib lub xeem lawm peb yeej sib yuav ua niam txiv tsis tau. Ua puas
txhais tau tias peb yog tib tsev neeg puag thaum ub los xwb.
Xaus lus tau tias peb lub xeem tseem muab peb faib sib nrug dua Ntsuab
thiab Dawb lawm, yog li kuv tsis paub hais tias peb tej tub txawj neeg
ntse niam no ho txawj ntse mus rau hom twg lawm, es thiaj lob ub lob
