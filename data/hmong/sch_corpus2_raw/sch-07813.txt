sub.  Tsuam tob yog qub lug xwb los yog lug tshab?

Tsuam tob puas yog tus pa tav kev ntawm qhov check point mas? Tam li
kuv imagine xwb mas nws yuav zoo li rab txiab uas muaj ib qhov sensor
ntsuas tib neeg txoj kev txhaum, kev lim hiam, yog leej twg muaj kev
txhaum ces thaum los hla rab tsuam tob ces sensor yuav txhom tau, yog
