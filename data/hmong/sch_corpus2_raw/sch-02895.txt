Muaj ib tug puj yug tau ob tug menyuam ntxaib, nwg muab ib tug hu ua
ooj (ounz) hab muab ib tug hu ua asmoos (Almon).  Tom qaab yug tau
cais nwg tsi muaj nyaj tu hab tau muab muag ib tug moog rua Thaibteb
lawm ho ib tug moog rua nyab laaj teb.  Tom qaab nuav tug ooj tau sau
ib dlaim ntawv hab nwg tug dluab lug rua leej nam kws yug nwg saib.
Thaum leej nam pum ooj dlaim dluab cais nwg txawm has tas klheev
asmoos xaa nwg dlaim dluab lug rua kuv saib hab mav.
Nwg tug quas yawg txhaj teb tas caag koj xaav pum amoos dlaim dluab
maj?
Tug quas puj teb tas vim kuv ncu ncu nwg hab xaav pum saib nwg zoo le
caag lawm.
Tug quas yawg teb tas koj pum ooj cais koj pum asmoos lawm los maj.
Tug quas puj teb tas vim le caag koj has le ntawm?  T
ug quas yawg teb tas koj tsi paub lov ob tug yog menyuam ntxaib, cais
ooj zoo le caag cais asmoos zoo le ntawm xwb los maj.
They are twince, when you see one, you see all.  (You see ounce, you
see almos)
Hmoob cais yog menyuam ntxaib xwb os koj pum thawj tug zoo le caag
cais tug thib ob yeej zoo le ntawm.  Thawj tug nom siv fwjchim hab
dlaag noj cais tug nom thib ob los yeej siv fwjchim hab dlaag noj ib
yaam rua qhov puab yog ntxaiv xwb.
