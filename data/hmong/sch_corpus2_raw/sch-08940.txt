Dear Tony,
Christianity is no doubt the bedrock hidden beneath the U.S.
Constitution and it has proven to be making progress for America, but
true christian principles as I have acquired or known them resemble
more of the following Hmong Shiyii fable:
The Crab and Its Mother (Roob Ris & Leej Niam)
A Mother CRAB said to her son, "Why do you walk so one-sided, my child?
It is far more becoming to go straight forward."  The young Crab
replied:  "Quite true, my dear Mother, if you will show me the straight
way, I promise I will walk in it."  The Mother tried in vain, and
submitted without remonstrance to the reproof of her child.
The theme is, example is more powerful than precept.

Yog lawm mas, the Christian principle yog ib qhov ncaaj kawg, kev ncaaj
nceeg, kev kaaj sab, kev zoo sab, hab kev hum xeeb.

another.  Cov kwv tij moob kws tseem coj qhub kev cai push to the limit
lawm ces kawg yuav tau has ncaaj has tas yog ua le caag tug ntseeg
txhaj le ua sab moog tseeg Vaajtswv lawm nua.
