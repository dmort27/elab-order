mus tom THamkrabok mus thov ntawv ua neeg tawg rog tom UNHCR office in
Bangkok coob kawg. Tabsi, UNHCR kom hmoob rov qab, txog caij, lawv mam
tuaj ntsib hmoob tom "zos qhov tsua".
Tabsi, vim hmoob tsis muaj tus qhia kom lawv paub qhov tseeb. Hmoob ua
raw lus moo cua, ua raws siab xav. Hnov qov twg, khiav qhov ntawd.
Hmoob tsis mloog kom tseeb tso mam ua.
Ntawm qhov 8,000 tus hmoob yuav tau tuaj ntawd, tsis muaj tseeb.
Tamsim no, luag twb tsi lees paub hais tias cov hmoob tim "zos qhov
tsua" yog neeg tawg rog lawm.
Ua ntej luag yuav tos ib pab neeg tawg rog tuaj, cov neeg ntawd yuav
tsum muaj ntaub ntawv ua neeg tawg rog raws txoj cai. Tamsim no, cov
hmoob tim qhov tsua zoo siab hais tias, lawv yuav tau tuaj yooj yim li
siab xav. Tabsi, lawv tsis paub hais tseem tshuav ntau yam sawv daws
yuav tsum tau sibzog sibpab hais saib luag puas yuav rov qab qhib.
The US Embassy thiab UNHCR yeej tsis tau lees paub qhov luag sibtham
yav tas los. Txawm luag paub los, luag yeej tsis lees, vim txhua yam
lawv sib tham yav tag los, tseem yog "PREMATURE" xwb. Qhov no, hais
tau tias, txhua yam luag hais tau lawm los, tseem yuav hloov tau. Yog
peb hmoob tsis ua zoo, lam tau lam ua, luag yuav rhuav luag cov lus li
cas los yeej tau. YOg peb txawj sib pab, mus hais kom yog txoj cai,
tej zaum luag tseem yuav qhib txoj kev rau peb ib zaug ntxiv. Hmoob
yuav tsum paub peb li limit, thiab ho yuav tsum paub peb txoj cai. yog
peb tsispaub  peb txoj cai nyob qhov twg, peb lam tau lam mus hais,
