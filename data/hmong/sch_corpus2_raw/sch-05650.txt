Phooj ywg Moob Lajleeb,
Koj hais tau ob sob lus ua piv txwv uas zoo kawg.
Piv txwv 1:
"Muab lub taub hau nrog lub taubhau" vs. "Muab lub taub hau nrog lub
taub hau"
Thaum sau ces sau li thawb kab no yeej zoo dua, tiam sis thaum hais ces
txawm hais li cas los peb yeej tsis hnov qhov txawv. Thiab nws yeej
tsis ua rau peb to tau yuam kev.
Piv txwv 2:
"Koj puas novqaab dlaabtsi le" vs. "Koj puas nov qaab dlaabtsi le" or
"Koj puas nov qaab dlaab tsi le"
Thaum sau ces sau li kab ib thiab kab ob zoo dua, tiam sis thaum hais
ces tag peb kab no yeej hais tib yam nkaus. Cov sob lus no mas yuav
hais li cas los peb yeej tseem to taub yuam kev taus. My correction
version is "Koj puas novqaab dabtsi li? = Did you forget something?"
thiab "Koj puas hnov qab dabtsi li = Do you taste it good at all?".
Txawm koj yuav sau ua ntawv los koj yuav hais ua lus, los tus mloog
thiab tus nyeem yeej tsis to taub yuam kev lawm.
