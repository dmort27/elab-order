Tus kwv Txawj cuab,
Has txug tej le nuav tes yeej tsis txawv dleb le os, yog tuab tug dlej
num le hab, taab kuas muaj ib nub thaib yeej yuav ntsib teeb meem xwb
xwb le los mas, thaib lub teb chaws nyob tau muaj 60 tawm xyoo lawm
tsis tau ua rog hlo le es thaib khaav theeb heev, kuas sis thaib twb
ntsib teeb meem taam sim nuav lawm, koj saib thaib lub teb chaws tes
yuav ua peb paab tuab neeg sib tua sib dlus huv tej nroog le ntaag
nawb, nplog twb nkaag ib ceg rau cov "thaib iv xaam" kom sib paab muab
nplog cov aav rov qaab lug ua nplog tug le qub dlua, le kws qub thaum
tug vaaj ntxwv "kuone boua rong" puab kaav teb chaws nawb, nplog lub
teb chaws tes taag nrho ntawm "Bangkok" ntawd tuab si le les es, kuv
yuav ua ntxoov tes ntsia lawv sib kom ceem zaum no mas yeej muaj ceem
xwb-b ntag laiv.
