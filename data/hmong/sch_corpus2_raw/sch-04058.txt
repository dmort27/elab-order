Yaiv, kuv tsis xav tias koj yog dr pao no es koj yog yawg lov. Hais li
ko ces peb paub zoo lawm mas, koj tsis tag yuav surrender los peb yeej
paub tias yuav under table xwb ces tau lawm lov mav.
Tseeb tiag, kuv pom tias lub niag zos SCH no tsam no tshuav
tsis yog lawv khiav tawm mus lwm qhov peb twb paub tias muaj ib cov
coob coob mask tuaj lawm xwb ov, koj los paub zoo kawg koj yeej tseg
tsis tau lub me zos sch no li nav, paub tias koj yuav busy heev vim
muaj ob chaw nyob lawm ne yuam, kav tsij mus take k me niam yaus tsam
ces ho lam muaj lwm tus tuaj take k lawm thiab laiv, nyuam qhuav yuav
tau tshiab tshiab txhob lam tso luag nyob luag pw txias to tsam ces
luag pauv siab lawm nawb.
