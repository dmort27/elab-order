To All My Hmong Internet Friends.
We, the Hmong in general were born to react, not to proact to thing.
Since when we will change our mentality to proact (or prevent) to thing
that is going to happen.
" Hmoob Dej Dawb should have been prevented many months or event a year
ago not to come to today reality".
"Peb Hmoob ib txwm ntseeg cov neeg hais lus cuav tshaj cov neeg hais
lus tseeb.  Vim li no peb thiaj li pom tej xwv txheej zoo li no".
Giving these Hmong people food and help them to get out of the
situation may not be a difficult thing to do . However, are we willing
to change our mentalility so that the hmong people will become a better
citizen to the country which they live, and to analyze thing closely
before making a decision to to thing.
"Txob mus tsawg $10.00 nyob sab ntuj dej tim ub.  Thov tuav koj $1.00
ntawm  tes kom ruaj rauj."
"Raws li kuv hno "RUMOR" hais mas vim muaj peb cov nyob sab nthuj deb
tim no pheej mus hais kom cov nyob sab ntuj deb tim ub tuaj vim peb cov
nyob tim no mas nyob tshav ntuj rig xwb hos lawv cov nyob tim ub mas ua
cas yuav nyob tsaus ntuj nti xwb.  Peb cov nyob tim no yuav tsum qhiav
qho tseeb rau lawv hais peb mas caum lub moos ua neej xwb -- twb tsis
muaj caij los nrog poj niam me muam noj mov ua ke li".
I hope that we will lean a lession by telling the truth and will change
to prevent/proact then react.
