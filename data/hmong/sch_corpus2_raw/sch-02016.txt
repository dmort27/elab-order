Yog peb xuas lub qhovncauj has xwb ces peb cov kws tuaj huv nuav
pestsawg leej ces yeej muaj txug npaum ntawd zaaj lug.  Tej lug ntawd
yog paajfuam kws yog kwsfuam rhawv tawm tuaj xwb.  Thaus has kuas muaj
puavpheej le b2bm has ces tej paajfuam ntawd dlhau moog ua fuabcua
taag lawm.  Dlhau le ntawd tug tuabneeg ntawd txawm haslug tsi muaj
kuab, moob los zoo tuabyaam le ntawd hab.
Le cov tuab tshawb txug tuabneeg lub laajxeeb, Carl Jung, yeej hastas
tej mivnyuas tseem yau yeej muaj tej fwvcheej ntawd, tamsis nwg kaav
ib ntus xwb.  Thaus nwg luj lawm ces tej lug nwg has ntawd kuj pluj
kuab zujzug moog lawm hab.
Lawvle Emanuel Swedenborg sau ntawv tseg rua txheej 17 ntawd, thaus
tug plig lug saab ntuj nrau-u lug rua saab qajntawv nraunuav, nwg ncu
txhuayaam rua saab ntuj ntawd.  Thaus nwg peemtsheej lug ua noobneej
lawd, nwg tug ntsujncu rua saab ntuj nrau-u foobqaab lawm.
yoglentawd, thaus xeevxwm ua tuabneeg lawm suavdlawg txhajle tsi paub
txug saab ntsujplig ntawd.  Nwg kuj hastas qho tug yeej tseem ncu txug
tej ntawd hab tamsis yog ib ntu thaus tseem yau ntawd xwb.
Thaus has muaj ntaubntawv lawd, nwg kuj muaj chaw moog hab.  Tamsis
ncauj dlawb has ncauj lug xwb ces, ib zag, ob zag taag, zag peb ces
yog lug fuabcua lawm xwb.  Moob yeej ibtxwm has, tamsis tsi muaj leej
twg has qhov tseeb.  Qhov tseeb kws puab, koj, losyog lwm has ces,
hastas, "Nov puab has."  Thaus koj nov puab has xwb ces yog lug fuab
lug cua xwb.
