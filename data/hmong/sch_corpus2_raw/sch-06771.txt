Chav, kuv xav tias Maiv Nas hais yog lawm, Phraya = "Lord", "Sir.",
"Duke" or Knight in the western world. Nyob ntawm tus neeg ntawd lub
laug hauj lwm thiab tej hauj lwm nws ua. Nyob li, Phabnyas, Tubnpis,
Tub liab thiab tub Txawg mas yuav yog Duke, tab sis GVP mas yuav yog
ib tug Knight lawm. Lawv cov no puav leej tau lub title Phaya los
ntawm fuabtais Lostsuas. Qhia tau tias, lawv puav leej tau ua hauj lwm
zoo rau lub teb lub chaw thiab fuabtais tej pejxeev. No long ago the
queen of England graint the title Sir. to Elton John the famouse
singer, because of many of his inspirational musics and songs.
Tab sis los lus phaya, phraya cov neeg Lostsuas thiab Thaib muab siv
rau ntau hom neeg, rau txawv caij nyoog, yog li lub ntsiab lus yuav
pauv raw cov context uas lawv siv. Muaj tej thaum kuj tau lub ntsiab
lus li yog "Wizard" thiab. e.g. Phraya Ine (Phab nyas eem).
Please note that, phayaa thiab aya (phab nyas thiab as nyas) tsis yog
tib yam/ tib los.
