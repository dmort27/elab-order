I can't speak for Dr. Paoze or Mong Fed.  I have never seen or met the

Xws le kuv to taub, Dr. Paoze yeej tsi faib peb haiv neeg Moob (Moob
Dlawb hab Moob Leeg) le.  Nwg tsuas has tas, Moob Leeg muaj Moob Leeg
lug hab kev cai dlaab qhuas; Moob Dlawb kuj muaj Moob Dlawb lug hab
kev cai dlaab qhuas.  (This is a fact!)  Yog le nuav, peb txawm yog
tuab tse Moob xwb los peb yeej yog ob paab tuab neeg tag tag le.  Peb
ob hom lug nuav tseem ceeb ib yaam nkaus (do you not agree with
this?).  Lug Moob Dlawb le feem coob peb puav leej txawj.
Qhov Dr. Paoze hab coob coob tug Moob Leeg ntshai mas tsom muaj ib nub
twg peb cov lug Moob Leeg pluj xwb.  Yog le caag?  Vim txhua yaam lug
hab ntawv sau tom tsoom fwv (gov't: school, social service, dept. of
motor vehicle, etc.) mas yog lug Moob Dlawb xwb.  Thaum kawg, luas
yuav tas peb tsuas paub lug Moob Dlawb xwb, yeej tsi muaj lug Moob
Leeg le.  Qhov tseeb, tej kev tsi caaj nceeg nuav tub tshwm ntev lug
lawm.  Piv txwv, kaum xyoo dlhau lug, kuv moog ntsug ib tug principal
qha ntawv thaam txug hauj lwm.  Nwg nug kuv tas, koj yog Moob Ntsuab
los Moob Dlawb?  Kuv thxa teb nwg tas kuv yog Moob Ntsuab.  Nwg txawm

txhua tes tsua yog Moob Leg los yog Ntsuab xwb.  Tej lug kws nwg nug
kuv nuav, kuv tsi ceeb vim tej teeb meem ntawd yeej muaj ntev ua ntej
ntawd lawm.
