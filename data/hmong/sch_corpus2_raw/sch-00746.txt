3.      yog li no koj tseem tsi tau nkagsiab zoo txog freedom…thaum tus
adult ntawd nws txaus siab nws tus kheej lawm.  Nws tsi yog, lwm tus
yuav muaj txoj cai mus cuam tshuam nws txoj kev nws ua lawm.  Yog koj
tseem hais tias, nws qhov freedom of choice no los physically or
mentally hurt others—yog li koj tseem nyog deb ntawm tebchaw America
no nawb.  Yog nws tsi yog rape or physical harm other person by force,
if s/he (adult) is willing to participate in his/her own choice and
rights, then no one shall infringe that…and that is why freedom really
is. Hmoob txoj kab li kev cai, nws tsi mus raw li coj laws of the land
that we live in…qhov no, vim yog peb hmoob ua li dev npua cai xwb, tsi
yog carefully evaluate to abide by the laws. Hmong creates its under
hmong’s own turn and define.
