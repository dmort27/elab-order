Nyob zoo tsoom Hmoob kwv tij;
Yog hais txog Hmoob lub neej ces tu tu lub siab ua luaj, puag hauv
nduab plawv rev nplooj ntsws.  Twb tau txhiab niaj pua tiam, hmoob
tseem cais koj pab kuv npawg, hmoob ub hmoob no.
Ua cas Hmoob sib hlub ua luaj, tiam sis yuav tsis txawj sib pab sib
hlub kom rub tau Hmoob nyob thoob ntiaj los thooj ib tsob koom ib
txoog.  Nej Hmoob tub txawj ntxhais ntse, puas muaj cob leej tswv yim,
tshib txhim peev xwm muaj txaus los txheeb neej Hmoob.  Neej Hmoob kom
nyob ruaj khov.  Niaj pua txhiab tag sim neej, Hmoog thiaj li nyob
nyaab xeeb tsis raug hmab suav caij tsuj ntsim siab kom plawv tsuav
nres.
Hmab sua muaj zoo tswv yim.  Tswv yim muab Hmoob cais ua pab kom muaj
pawg.  Thaum no Hmoob mam li rov tua, ntxeev, thiab rhuv nws tsob
Hmoob.  Yog Hmoob rhuav Hmoob thiaj puas ntsoog.  Hmab Suav yeej rhuav
tsis tau Hmoob.
Cov Kwv Tij Hmoob neej tsa, thoob luaj pua, leej twg muaj zoo tswv yim,
thov qhia Hmoob paub.  Kom nej peb Hmoob thiaj tsis cib nyeej tsib toom
faib pab cais pawg, txhuas ntxiv lawm.
Tus sau:  Txooj Cag Hmoob

Txooj Cag Hmoob:
Sim ho tuaj taws koj lub teeb ntxiv saib peb puas pom kev deb zog.  Qav
kaws tes taw ces kawg lino lawm xwb las.

Nyaj yuav tsis txaus tshai npaum koj hais voj? Nyaj yuav yog H'moob
criticize H'moob xwb. Koj muab xav li no, "I rather be criticized by
the wise than be praised by the fool."
