Ua tsaug Thorn txog koj nug txoh HAIS.
Hais yog ib lub organisation uas tsa rau hnub 18th September 2004, los
pab ua ncauj-ua lus txog tej teebmeem Hmoob nyob txhua-txhua qhov.
Nws yuav ua haujlwm nrog txhua lub koomhaum.  Muaj tej qhov yog tias
ho tsis muaj ib lub koomhaum hais txog, es HAIS ho pom tias yuav vtsum
tau tawm ncauj-lug thiab no ces tejzauk HAIS yuav hais txog tej ntawv
thiab.  Muaj tej zaum ho muaj tus tib neeg xav kom HAIS ua advocay los
HAIS mam saib seb yuav pab tau dabtsi thiab.
Lus Avkis ces yog tias, HAIS yob ib lub "non-profit, non-political and
multinational organization focused on responding to broad range of
issues in support of the Hmong people of the world.
HAIS is dedicated to provide education, information, advocacy services
and support.
HAIS is supported by volunteers from across the globe who come from
different backgrounds and beliefs but all share a common focus: to
seek ways to ENHANCE the life of the Hmong people no matter where they
live; and desire that the Hmong can fully participate in and integrate
into the country they live; and that they may live in peace and
prosperity with the same freedom, equality, and justice that is their
right as members of the International Community."
