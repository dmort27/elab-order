1.  make the rules/laws and obey them accordingly.  yus tsa tau txoj
cai los yog thaum muaj txoj cai nyob rau ntawd lawm, yus yuav tsum hwm
thiab ua raws li txoj cai.  yog yus ua txhaum txoj cai lawm yus yuav
tsum tau los lees txhaum thiab txaus siab hlo los nyob lub txim raws
li txoj cai teev tseg.
2.  compromise - koj muab koj ib qhov pov tseg, kuv muab kuv ib qho
pov tseg kom sawv daws tau koj ib qhov thiab kuv ib qho.  tau li no
xwb los yuav tsum zoo siab hlo tias twb tau lawm vim sawv dawv tau ua
ke.
3.  go by majority rule - feem coob pom zoo li cas lawv ces sawv daws
yuav tsum tau los yuav thiab ua li ntawd.
