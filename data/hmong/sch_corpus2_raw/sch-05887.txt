Yog li koj hais lawm.  Hmoob tsis ua teb noj lawm ces sawv daws tsis
tos lub tsiab peb caug npaum li yav thaum ub lawm.  Niam no, hmoob ua
hauj lwm to tsev hauj lwm lawm, txog caij ntuj so, sawv daws xav so los
ua kev lom zem.  Tis npe tshiab rau lub neej tshiab no los haj yam zoo.
Summer festival does not equal to New Year Celebration.
