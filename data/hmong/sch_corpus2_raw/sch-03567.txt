Last week I went to a Hmong meeting in Georgia and heard a lot of
Hmong-Glish during the conversation. They were good word, but could be
losing our Tongues very soon in the future. Below are some example. If
you have any more word. Please, add its to the sample.
Hmoob-English.
1.Thov nej chov hluas Yua tsum sib pab nrhiav ib lub ( Idea ) los nrog
kho Hmoob lub neeg kom xwm lis neeg ntejteb thiab.
2. Niaj hnub Nim no mas Chov tub txawj ntxshais nste mas ( stciky )
tsaj plaws.
3. Txiv neej hmoob American mas  ( stupid) heev tsua pom mus tim nplog
xwb.
4. Chov Miv ntxhais ntse nim no mas yeej tsis ( listen ) nws niam lis.
5. Bye, bye nej txhuas leej es mam sib ntsib dua nawb.
Hmoob-Lao-Tongues.
1. ( Pais ) zoo nej sawvdaws nawb.
2. Nej pos noj ( Padek-pam lem rau Tam Xoum) Papaya Salad.
3. Hniaj tso ntom tau mam ( Khib Suab tsaj) plaws.
4. Thov kom hnub no cov kev phem kev tsis zoo mus poob tag rau tim
( Vemcham=-vientiane ).
