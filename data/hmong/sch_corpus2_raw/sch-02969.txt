Hmoob yeej sib pab thiab sib khiav dej num li koj hais tiag tiam sis
Hmoob tsis yog ua pub dawb pub do = unconditionally. Example - koj
tuaj kuv txiv lub funeral & pab nyiaj txiaj mas kuv yuav tsum nco
ntsoov tuaj pauj koj ntsig.  Kuv tuaj koj ib lub rooj tshoob & nqa
nyiaj txiaj tuaj pab koj lawm mas kuv yuav xam ntsoov seb txog kuv
thib koj puas tuaj.  Yog yus tsis pauj ces luag yeej dub muag thiab
hais yus plig ncahv yus moo rau luag cuab kwv tij ntag nawb.  So
Hmong's giving is "conditional" and it's a system that serves Hmong
well so far.  Qhov downfall mas yog peb tej kev sib pab no peb xaiv
ntsej xaiv muag pab.  Yog tus peb xav tias yuav pauj tsis tau peb
ntsig ces peb pab tsawg (ib txhia hais tias pov qhov ntuj no nawb).
Tsim nyog tiag mas yuav tsum pab tus pluag ntau es tus muaj lawm ces
tsis tas pab li no mas yuav yog dua.
Charity raws li kuv opinion xwb mas yog pub unconditionally to those
in need with no expectation of any return. In this respect, we still
have a long way to go and I am hopeful that we will get there with our
younger generation. Yog peb txawj cob peb cov phaum hluas no ces peb
