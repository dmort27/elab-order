Tsis ntev los no, kuv twb tau tshaj tawm me ntsis txog peb cov hmoob
tau mus koom kev sablaj nrog cov nom tswv nplog liab thiab Koom haum
saib xyuas kev ncaj ncees nyob Geneva, Switzerland thaum lub
2-21-22-2005. Kuv ntseeg hais tias nej coob tus yuav paub zoo hais tias
zaum no yog peb mus tham txog dabtsi tiag. Peb coj tau cov lus hais
txog tsoom fwv nplog liab tua peb cov menyuam hmoob, tsim txom peb cov
hmoob nyob nplog teb, thiab tsis muaj kev ncaj ncees rau pejxeem hmoob
mus hais rau koom haum ntiaj teb. Cov nom tswv saib xyuas kev ncaj
ncees muaj 18 leej tau tuaj mloog saib co nom tswv nplog liab yuav teb
sawv daws tej lus li cas.
Txawm peb hmoob yuav chim thiab hais npaum li cas, thiab cov nom tswv
saib xyuas kev ncaj ncees yuav hais nyhav npaum li cas los, cov nom
tswv nplog yeej tsis lees hais tias lawv tau tsim txom hmoob, thiab
lawv yeej tsis lees hais tias lawv tau tua tsib tug menyuam hmoob thaum
lub 5hli xyoo 2004 ntawd.
Cov nom nplog tsuashais tias, yog sawv daws pheej liam hais tias nplog
tua Hmoob, ua cas ho tsis muaj neeg mus hais rau cov nom nplog li. Tsis
tas li ntawd, tus Governor hmoob nyob Xiengkhouang twb yog hmoob, tus
thawj tub rog nyob rau sab ntawd los yog Hmoob. Kom Hmoob mus nug saib
puas yog nkawv xa tub rog mus tua hmoob xwb.
Xyoo no, Nom tswv nplog sau tau lawv tsab annual report tuaj mus rau
tim UN. Daim report hais zoo heev. Luag hais tias, txhua haiv neeg nyob
rau lub tebchaws nplog muaj vajhaum sibluag, lawv yeej hlub txhua tus
pejxeem ib yam. Qhov luag kom cov neeg nyob toj siab tsiv los rau nram
chaw qis mas yog luag yuav txhim kho lub tebchaws kom zoo thiab muaj
noj haus ntxiv xwb.
Thaum sibtham xauslub rooj sablaj, cov nom tswv tim UN xaus lus hais
tias, zaum no tsoom fwv nplog teb tsis tau lawv cov lus nug li, hais
txog txoj kev tsim txom pejxeem nyob nplog teb. Tabsi, kom lwm zaus,
cov nom tswv nplog yuav tsum hais kom meej tshaj zaum no saib lawv txoj
kev tswjhawm pejxeem kev noj haus nyob nplog teb muaj li cas tiag. Tsis
tas li ntawd, cov nom tswv xaus lus hais tias, kom txhua tus pejxeem
nrog rau cov nom tswv nyob hauv lub tebchaws nplog yuav tsum sib hlub,
sib pab, nrog txhim ko lub tebchawsnplog rau sawv daws nyob, vim lub
tebchaws nplog yog sawv dawslub. Cov pejxeem nplog tsis muaj dua lwm
lub tebchaws, tsis muaj dua ib daim av, tsis muaj dua ib qho chaw rau
lawv mus nyob, tsuas yog lawv daim av nplog thiaj yog lawv sawv daws
lub tebchaws lawm xwb. Mas yog li, qhov yuav daws tau cov teebmeem hauv
lub tebchaws nplog ces yuav tsum yog sawv daws los koom tes ua ib ke,
National Reconciliation thiaj yog qhov yuav daws tau teebmeem lawm xwb.
Dhau mus, kuv yuav tham me ntsi txog cov kwvtij hmoob ua khiav los coob
coob rau Thaib teb. Tsis ntev los no, kuv twb tshaj tawm rau xov tooj
cua Radio Chiengmai thiab rau tebchaws America no hais tias tsoom Fwv
America tsis muaj kev qhib txais tos peb cov hmoob tim Thaib teb tuaj
mus America teb lawm. Tabsi, peb cov kwv tij hmoob tseem muaj kev cia
siab hais tias yog khiav tuaj txog Thaib teb, tuaj mus sau npe tag, ces
yuav tau tuaj mus America teb raws li sawv daws hnov. Mas yog li, sawv
daws thiaj tso vaj tso tsev tseg, muab nyiaj pua kev tuaj mus ua lub
neej txom nyem heev nyob rau Thaib teb txog rau niaj hnub no.
Vim yog muaj coob leej ntau tus tau hu tuaj rau peb koomhaum Hmong
International Human rights Watch. Lawv xav paub saib qhov hmoob khiav
coob coob los rau Thaib teb ntawd, puas yog America qhib kev txais tos
hmoob dua ib zaug ntxiv tiag. Lub 3-2-2005, peb koom haum HIHRW thiaj
tau nrog the State Department thiab UNHCR sibtham saib yog vim li cas
Hmoob thiaj khiav los coob ua luaj rau pem Zos Dej Dawb. Luag cov nom
tswv hais meej hais tias America tsis tau tso cai rau leejtwg mus qhib
ib qho chaw nyob qhov twg sau npe rau hmoob, vim America tsis muaj kev
qhib txais tos hmoob ntxiv lawm. Lub 3-4-2005, the State Department
thiaj sau ib tsab ntawv tawm hais txog qhov uas sawv daws hnov hais
tias yog leejtwg xav mus America teb, yuav tau mus sau npe pem zos Dej
Dawb. The State Department hais tias qhov no yog lam hais xwb. Vim
America tsis tau qhib kev txais tos hmoob ntxiv. Hnub 3-7-2005, the
State Department sau dua ib tsab ntawv tawm hais dua ib zaug ntxiv hais
tias, yog leejtwg hnov txog hais tias America tseem yuav qhib kev txais
tos Hmoob tuaj ntxiv, kom hais mus rau the State Department paub. Lub
3-9-2005, the State Department sau ib tsab ntawv tuaj rau peb koom haum
hais tias, qhov sawv daws hnov hais tias muaj kev sau npe nyob pem zos
Dej Dawb mas tsis yog the US Government, tsis yog the United Nations,
thiab tsis yog Lajthab npas Thaib qhib sau npe rau hmoob. Tsis tas li
ntawd, America tsis tau tso cia rau ib lub koom haum twg mus teem chaw
rau Thaib teb, los yog lwm qhov chaw sau npe rau Hmoob. Lawv twb hais
mus rau tag nrog cov agencies ua haujlwm rau cov neeg tawg rog, thiab
cov nom tswv xws li US Congress lawm hais tias tej yam no yog lam hais
xwb.
Tej lus no, tsisyog kuv muab coj los hais tsum lwm pab lwm pawg, lwm
tus neeg lub homphiaj. Tabsi vim yog kuv hlub txog peb cov kwvtij hmoob
uas txom nyem tabsi tsis paub qhov tseeb. Thaum lawv hnov tej yam li
no, txawm yuav muaj tseeb thiab cuav, los lawv cia li tso vaj tso tsev
tseg, siv nyiaj siv txiaj pua kev los mus ua lub neej txom nyem rau
Thaib teb. Kuv thiaj tshawb nrhiav kom tau qhov tseeb coj los qhia sawv
daws saib puas pab tau peb cov hmoob ua ntej lawv yuam kev mus rau txoj
kev txom nyem. Txawm kuv yuav hlub sawv daws npaum licas los, yog sawv
daws tsis mloog kuv hais, ces kuv yuav pabtsis tau.
Tamsim no, tseem muaj dua ib co lus yuav ua rau peb cov hmoob yuam kev
coob, yog hais tias sawv daws tsis mloog li kuv hais.
Lub 4-4-2005 tag los no, the State Department sau tau ib daim annual
report hais txog lawv txoj kev qhib txais tos neeg tawg rog nyob sab
East Asia tuaj rau America teb rau xyoo 2005 no. Daim report hais tias,
xyoo 2005 no, America yuav tos li 13,000 tus neeg tawg rog tim Thaib
teb tuaj, mas 10,000 tus ntawm 13,000 tus no yog Hmoob, dua li yog ces
yog nyablaj, phabmab, thiab lwm haiv neeg. Lub 4-7-2005, Radio Free
Asia tau muab daim report no coj los tshaj tawm hais tias, tamsim no,
cov hmoob tuaj txog America teb muaj 8080 leej lawm, mas America tseem
yuav tos li 10,000 tus hmoob tuaj ntxiv rau xyoo 2005 no. Qhov no, ua
rau peb cov hmoob yuam kev coob tus. Sawv daws xav hais tias yog li mas
tejzaum cov hmoob pem zos Dej Dawb yuav muaj feem tuaj raws li hais.
Thaum kuv hnov Radio Free Asia tshaj tawm li no, thiab muaj nej coob
tus tau hu tuaj nug saib peb koom haum Hmong International Human Rights
Watch puas paub txog cov xov xwm no. Kuv thiaj nrog the State
Department sib tham rau hnub Thursday lub 4hli tim 7, 2005 thaum sawv
ntxov. Vim kuv xav paub hais tias cov lus Radio Free Asia tshaj tawm
ntawd yog muab qhov twg los.
Raws li the State Department hais, cov lus sau tawm ntawm State
Department daim report rau lub 4-4-2005 no yog cov report lawv muab xa
mus rau The US Congress hais txog lawv lub fiscal year 2005 uas yog pib

tus neeg tawg rog ntawd yog cov hmoob hauv Wat Thamkrabok uas twb

The State Department lub fiscal year 2004 xaus rau lub 9-30-2004. Ua
ntej lub 9-30-2004 mas peb cov kwvtij hmoob tim Thamkrabok nyuam qhuav
tuaj txog li 4000-5000 tus xwb. Tseem tshuav 10,000 tawm tus. Cov tau

lawm.
Thov nej hais meej meej rau nej cov txheeb ze hais tias, yuav ua
dabtsi, yuav tau nug kom tau qhov tseeb thiaj tsis raug lwm tus dag,
thiaj tsis yuam kev mus rau txoj kev txom nyem ntxiv.
Tham ntxiv mus, nej muaj coob tus hu tuaj nug hais tias, ua li qhov nej
hnov hais tias twb muaj ib co hmoob mus xamphaj tom Bangkok, thiab yuav
tau tuaj sai sai no, ua peb koom haum puas paub.
Peb koom haum paub meej hais tias cov mus xamphaj nyob Bangkok mas tsis
yog cov mus sau npe rau pem zos Dej Dawb. Lawv yog cov uas cov txheeb
ze tim no ua ntawv mus tos lawv cov kwvtij uas yeej muaj teebmeem thiab
qualified ua neeg tawg rog xwb. Peb tsis paub hais tias, cov no muaj
pes tsawg yim, tabsi yuav tsis coob. Tejzaum tsuas muaj 2-3 yim xwb.
Ua ntej nej yuav ntseeg lwm tus hais tias yog muaj tus ub tus no muaj

Bangkok es lawv thiaj tau tuaj, nejyuav tu nug lawv tsev neeg kom paub

ntseeg lwm tus neeg dag hais tias nim yog muaj nws es cov hmoob tom
Bangkok thiaj tau tuaj. Mas ntshe yuav them nyiaj rau tus ub tus no kom
nws pab saib nej tsev neeg puas tau tuaj. Tsis muaj ib tug neeg twg
yuav hla dhau txoj cai.
Kawg no, kuv thov tshaj tawm rau nej sawv daws hais tias, nag hnmo muaj
