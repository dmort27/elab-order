Noj tsab yog moob lub neej ua teb noj, khwv 12 lub hlis es su ib lub
lim tam lug su kws yog tua tsaj txhu noj hab caws kwv tij ntsuab zej
ntsuab zog tuaj koom noj.  30 yog kev foom koob moov los yog ncu txug
ntuj, fuab tais, puj yawm tej koob moov kws khwv tau noj ru xyoog
ntawd.  Yaam tsi zoo muab pov rua tom qaab, yaam zoo kuas muaj ntxiv
rua xyoo tshab.
Yog le ntawd, 30 xyoo rua meskas teb nuav lawm, yog "Koob Moov" kws peb
tau tuaj nyob rua lub teb chaw vaam meej.  Khwv, ua num, tau noj tau
haus ces yuav tau tseg ib lub lim tam lug ncu txug tej koob moov kws
tau txais ntaud.  Koob moov lug qhov twg lug?
