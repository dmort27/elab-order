Qhov peb yws koj mas vim peb txhawb (support) koj xwb.  Koj yuav hnav
tiab ntshai kuv yuav tsis pub vim koj twb to taub zoo lawm.  Koj cov
lus nug kuj muaj ntau yam zoo nyob hauv kawg.  Txhob xav tias peb yws
koj es peb yuav lauj koj ko taw.  Lwm tus mas kuv kuj tsis paub, tiam
sis kuv ib tug ua yuav txhawb koj as long as your vision is to
help/improve the life of our fellow hmong as a whole and always search
the truth. Txawm pab tsis tau nyiaj txiag los tsuav txhawb koj ib lo
lus qhuav xwb los tej zaum yuav zoo koj siab ua koj tej dej num.
Nco ntsoov tias yuav ua ib tug thawj coj, yeej mag sawv daws muab
yws/cem's tej thaum koj yeej xav tso lub nyuag nra pov tseg tom qab ha
hos.  Tiam sis tsuas xav tias yus twb kawm txawj kawm ntse los, yog yus
tseem ntaus siab me tag npaum ces yus tseem ruam tshaj cov niag cem cem
yus.  Koj tsis ntseeg kuv no koj sim mus nug hmoob cov ua thawj coj

ntiv tes es puas yog mas.
