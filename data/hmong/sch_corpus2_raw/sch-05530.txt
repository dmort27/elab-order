Generally Hmong tsi sib hlub-txawj sib hlub
what you mentioned on your post is purely a looking for favor in
return. That is not hlub. to hlub one must give and give from the
bottom of the heart and do not look for return in favor.
here is what kuv txiv hais rau kuv.
Yog koj muaj tus twm laij liaj mas koj tus kwvtij losis phoojywg uas
muaj twm laij liaj or nees thauj nraa yuav offer to qiv nws tus rau koj
siv. Tiamsis yog koj tsis muaj mas koj mus qiv nws yeej tsi kam.
The reason being is when your friend needs, he may be able to borrow
yours.
