hastas yog dlaag nua hab ne.  Ua caag ho tsi hastas "taub dlaaj" hos

"Dlaaj Dlees kev khaub pleeg nkauj nraug zoo npaum nuav"....how about

I think ruaqhov Moob cov lug is a tonal language so lub suab twg
ncaaj rua qhov ntawd es zoo noog hab sib dlho ces that is what it is.
Lawv le kuv nkaagsab peb lug Moob mas lus lug "dlaaj" hab "dlaag" mas
has the same meaning yog peb siv lug has txug kev dlaag.  Tej thaum
kws peb sib thaampem mas yog yug tsi ntseeg nwg pestsawg mas yug kheev-
v hastas "ntshe koj has txug tug dlaaj-dlaaj xwb pob?"
Xim "dlaaj" los peb Moob kuj siv lug pev txug yaam kws moog seem rua
saab negative los yog tsi zoo hab xwsle...."dlaaj ntseg" in English
"pale" for a not so healthy looking person.  Another example kws Moob
siv yog xim dlub as "positive" like "koj has dlaaj los dlub mas" which
translate "negative or positive" on the subject.
