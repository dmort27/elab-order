Npaj:
·	Muab cov txhuvnplaum kib kom dajdaj lossis kom brown tsimtsawv.
Muab tsaws cia kom txias tsimtsawv lawm mamli muab zom kom mos
tsimtsawv rawsli qhov yus nyiam.  Yog siv tsis tag, muab ntim cia rau
tej lub lamhwj cia tau siv rau lwm zaus.
·	Muab cov noob zaubtxhwbqaib kib kom brownish losyog dubtsawv es muab

nplaum.
·	Muab ob lub mag dos liab uas luaj li txiv aple los txhoov kom
metsawv tso tseg cia.
·	Tev li ob peb lub qij tseg cia.
o	Muab cov qij thiab cov mag dos txhoov qhwv uake rau hauv ntawv ci
thiab muab ci rau saum qhovcub lossis txhob muab qhwv thiab ciali muab

thaum muagmuag lawm mamli muab tsaws kom txias.
o	Muab zom kom mosmos lossis muab tuav thiab muab daus cia.
·	Muab dos ntsuab, zaubtxhwb, pumhub, thiab luamlaws tsaug kom txhob
muaj suabzeb thiab de lossis txhoov kom luv li 1inch tej.
·	Muab li 1 lossis 2 pounds nqaij ntshiv npua lossis nyuj hlais ua tej
daim 2 x 1 inches es muab suam nyiasnyias lossis muab zom kom mostsawv
cia.
·	Muab cov plabnyuj hau kom siavsiav thiab muab hlais tej daim ntev li
ob yag tes thaib nyiasnyias tseg cia.
·	Mub ib daim plab nyuj luaj li ib xib tej coj los hau kom siav thaib
muab thau kom txias.  Muab nws suam tej daim nyiasnyias ntev li ob yag
tes tseg cia.
