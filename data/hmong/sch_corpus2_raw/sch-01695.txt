Cov Phoojywg Tub Yos Tsiaj,
Twb yuav rov txog lub caij peb mus plob havzoov tua muaslwj dua lawm.
Nyob rau WI yog lub xeev uas peb tau muaj tej xwm txheej tsis zoo los
raug ntau zaus rau lub caij yos. Zaum xwmtxheej uas loj tshaj uas
novqab tsis tau kiag yom zaum Nchiav Suav tau tua cov tub yos tsiaj
tuag lawm 6 leej. Yog Nchaiv Suav lam kawm kom paub thajtsam yos kom
zoo, yog Nchaiv Suav lam ua siab ntev zog, es nyaj tej xwm txheej no
yuav tsis tshwmsim loj npaum ntawd.
Yog li kuv thov nqua hu peb ib tseem tubyos kom sawvdaws yuav tau
xyuamxim, yuav tau rub lub siab kom ntev, yuav tau kawm kom paub thaj
chaw yus yos kom zoo zoo. Dhau li ntawd, yuav tau kawm kom paub tus
cai yos havzoov lossis yos muaslwj kom zoo zoo, thiaj tsis ua txhaum
luag txoj cai.
Kawm kom paub koj thaj chaw yos kom zoo (Visit this DNR map site):
http://dnr.wi.gov/org/land/facilities/dnr_lands_mapping_search.asp?
Kawm kom paub txojcai yos kom zoo (See Hmong version hunting
regulation).
http://dnr.wi.gov/org/land/wildlife/regs/Deer10_hmong.pdf
Good luck and hunt safely,
HL
