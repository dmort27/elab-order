Hmoob Lao
Tug Quixing Lee lub nteeg cais puab tub kuam kuv moog preach rua
ntaag.  Kuv pum tas luag neeg America mas ua tau zoo heev le, yog koj
tuaj koom mas koj yuav pum.  Luag yeej muaj ob tug tub rog, ib tug
nyob saab peg taub hau, ib tug nyob saab nraag kua taw.  Txhua txhua
10 nasthis mas luag hloov ob tug tshab.  Peb Hmoob yeej tsi tau muaj
ib lub nteeg kws yuav raug honor npaum yawg le os.
Thaum yawg Quixing lub theeg ntawm kuv kuj pum Lo Lee tom Appleton
tuaj koom hab tabsis tsi pum Ying B Lee tuaj.  Qha tau has tas txhua
yaam yeej zoo heev le.  Muaj ib yawg tub rog Hmoob Xyooj tuaj muab
honor rua hab has tas txhua tug tub rog Hmoob kws paab America tuag
yeej tsi tau lub nteeg zoo npaum Quixing lub le nua os.
Tom qaab peb lug tsev mas coob tug has tas yog tuag rua lub tebchaws
es tau lub nteeg zoo hab muaj meej mom npau Quixing mas puab yeej zoo
sab tuag hab nua tag.  Kuv xaav tas yeej zoo heev le nawb.
