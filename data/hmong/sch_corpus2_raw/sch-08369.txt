parents, as in one form of their roots. Teev niam teev txiv, not as far
as teev pog/ yawg, because if your dad is still alive you will never
get to do it, until either your dad or mom pass away.  After you serve
your dad the treat before asking your mom, then you go around with his
closest brothers and relatives down the line.  And, no way will you
call upon the ladies side as you are serving them food, or your pog
your yawg. Therefore, they are only going back to your parents who gave
you birth to be here as human being.... smile! tsi tag li ntawv thiab,
