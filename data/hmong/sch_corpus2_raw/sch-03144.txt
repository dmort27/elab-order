ho yuav muaj ntaus qib (how many grade levels?) thiab yuav muab cov

knowlege, KIPP, ASSE International Exchange Studies, etc...)?

los kom lawv nyiam thiab pom zoo tuaj koom kev qhia kev kawm tib si...
In order for us to progress with the world this Hmong language/culture
school has to mix with at least one other world language and culture
as well; i.e. Hmong/Chinese, Hmong/Spanish, Hmong/French, Hmong/
Japanese, Hmong/Korean, etc.
Npaj tos nej li laj lim tswv yim txhawb...

yuav tsum ntseeg no xwb; peb yuav tsum xyaum kom paub txog peb kev cai
