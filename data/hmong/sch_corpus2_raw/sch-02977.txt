2. Punctuation mark: cim
Period (.) = cim nres
Kuv mua tus cim no tis npe ua "cim nres" vim muab txhais ncaj qha los
ntawm lo lus "full stop".
Comma (,) = cim ncua
Kuv mua tus cim no ti npe ua "cim ncua" vim thaum yus hais lus tag ib
kab, yus ncua me ntsis mam rov hais kom tag sob lus. Pivtxwv: Yog koj
hlub kuv ib leeg xwb tiag tiag, kuv yuav tso plhuav kuv lub neej, lub
cuab tam, thiab txoj sia rau koj ib leeg.
Question mark (?) = cim nug
Tus cim no los yeej cia li hu raws nws lub ntsiab xwb.
Exclamation mark (!) = cim seev
Kuv muab tus cim no tis npe hu ua cim seev, vim thaum yus hais tej lo
lus uas nws muaj lub suab seev ces mam siv xwb. Pivtxwv li: ntuj e!,
uaj! ab!, haub yau!
Colon (:) = cim nov
Kuv muab nws ti npe hu ua cim nov vim hais tias peb tsuas siv thaum
peb hais lo lus li nov (li no), xws li no, nram qab no, pivtxwv li
no ....
Semi-colon (;) = cim txuas
Kuv muab tus cim no hu ua cim txuas vim hais tias nws siv los muab ob
sob lus coj los txuas ua ib sob. Pivtxwv li no: Thov koj hlub kuv ib
leeg xwb tiag tiag nawb; kuv yuav tso plhuav kuv lub neej, lub cuab
tam, thiab txoj sia rau koj ib leeg thiab xwb od!
Quotation mark (" ") = cim qog
Tos kuv muab hu ua cim qog no los vim peb siv rau thaum yus qog lwm
tus cov lus, xws li: Hmoob-Lao nyiam siv cov lus hais tias "phoojywg",
hos Txawj vam yias mas ho nyiam siv lo lus hais tias "peb yog ib".
Apostrophe mark (') = cim hla
Kuv muab tis npe hu ua cim hla vim hais tias siv rau thaum yus hla ob-
peb tug ntawv es tsis sau kom tag. Nyob rau tamsis no mas kuv tsis tau
pom Hmoob siv tus cim no li, tabsis nws yuav raug siv rau yav tom ntej
no.
Dash (-) = cim kes
Tus cim kes no los cia li hu raws li qhov nws kes ib kab xwb.
Hyphen (_) = cim kesqis
Tus cim kesqis no los cia li hu raws li qhov nws kes ib kab qis qis
xwb. Tus cim no mas Hmoob tsis tshuav siv, tabsis yav tom ntej no yeej
yuav siv thiab.
Slash (/) = cim kem
Tus cim no kuv muab hu ua cim kem no vim peb siv nws los kem ob lo
lus, xws li: pojnim/txivneej etc....
Parentheses ( ) = cim thaiv
Kuv muab hu ua cim thaiv vim hais tias nws thaiv tom hauv ntej thiab
thaiv tom qab.
Bracket [ ] = cim sua
Kuv muab hu ua sua vim hais tias nws sua tom hauv ntej thiab sua tom
qab los ua ib kev.
Braces { } = cim puag
Kuv muab hu ua cim puag vim hais tias nws puag tag nrho rau hauv
plawv. Pivtxwv li kab zauv nram qab no:
{[4(2+5)]-2}/2 = 13
