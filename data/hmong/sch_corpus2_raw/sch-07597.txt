Tam li Hmoob ib txwm hais/siv mas peb lus Hmoob yeej tsis muaj kev pauv
hloov qauv cev ntawm lus Hmoob li nad. Piv txwv los lus "mus" ("to
go")":
Past Tense = twb/tau mus/moog (went); siv ua kab lus: kuv twb/tau mus
kawm ntawv.
Present tense = mus/moog (go); siv ua kab lus: kuv mus kawm ntawv.
Future tense = mam/yua mus/moog (will go); siv ua kab lus: kuv mam/yuav
mus kawm ntawv.
