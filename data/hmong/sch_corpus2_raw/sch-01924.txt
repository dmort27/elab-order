Caijnyoog – tense-time (npe) qhatxug hastas nwg yog yaav taaglug,
taamsim nuav, losyog yaav pegsuab.
Faabtawgntso – going on (npe) qhatxug hastas ib tug tuabneeg tseem
taabtom leg ib teg dlejnum rua lub caijnyoog taamsim ntawd.
Hloovnpe – pronoun (npe) qhatxug ib lulug kws siv lug hloov lub npe
tuabneeg, npe nroog, losyog tej npe kws nchaa moo ntawd chaw.
Ibtxwm coj – tradition (npe) qhatxug hastas ib paab tuabneeg yeej nyob
lubneej zoo lentawd ib tam dlhau moog rua ib tam.
Keebkwm – history (npe) qhatxug lub hauvpaug ntawm ib tug, ib paab,
losyog ib haivtuabneeg lug ntawm nwg lub chaw yug, thaaj chaw nrhavnoj
nrhavhaus, hab lub chaws xaus ntawm lubneej.
Khoomsiv – object (npe) qhatxug hastas nwg yog tej tswjsiv xws le
taig, dlav, rooj, tog, vaajtsev, hab tsheb.
