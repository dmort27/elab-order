All Hmongs,
This is the greatest news of the year for our people, the Hmong. Any
of you who have relatives in the refugee camp, please tell them the
truth about life in such a great country. I strongly believe that the
US is the country we, the Hmong always dream about.
Peb Hmoob ib txheej dhau ib txheej, ib tis dhau ib tis, tsuas xav kom
peb tau nyob rau lub ntuj tiag teb tus (peace and freedom) thiab tej
laus ib txwm foom kom rau peb sawv daws kom peb ua me los kom tau
ntau, ua ntau los kom noj tsis tag, noj tsis tag kom tau coj mus ntiav
dag, noj tsis txog kom tau coj mus ntiav zog, tus tsis ua los kom tau
noj, kom noj tsis coos haus tsis nqhis, noj tsis txob hnav tsis
txhawj. Niaj hnub no peb cov uas tuaj nyob rau teb chaws no ces tej
lus uas tej laus ib txwm foom no yeej los rau peb sawv daws lawm.
Thov qhiab rau cov nyob tom qab kom lawv kav tsij tuaj nrog peb koom
tej kev tau zoo no thiab mog. Raws li kuv nco mas tuaj txog teb chaws
no kuv yeej tsis tau muaj ib zaug uas yuav nqis nqhis ib yam zaub mov
twg uas yus yuav npau suav toog noj li thaum tseem nyob ntuj qub qab
lawm. Thiab kuv yeej tsis nco txob tsis nco txhawj txog kev kwv dej
txiav taws, kev ntxhua kahub nciv khaub ncaws hlo li lawm vim peb ua
me los tau ntau es peb thiaj tau coj los ntiav dag ntiav zog lawm. (Ua
zog thaib teb 50 Baht per day ($.08/hr), ua zog teb chaws no $50 per
day ($7/hr))
Thov txhob zais siab rau cov nyob tom qub qab mog.
