rua nruab ntawv tuab si. Puab hu Vaaj Tswv Ntuj, taab sis tseem hlawv

txuj cai txawv. Has txug kev ua hluas nkauj hluas nraug, yog thaum tug

lawd, nam txiv hab cov thawj coj yuav nrug ob tug hluas ntawd tsom xaam
saib suav dawg puas pum zoo rua ob tug sib hlub. Yog pum zoo mas suav
