Li kuv twb hais los lawm, thawj sob lus yog paj lug, sob lus 2 yog
ntsiab lug, uas txhais thawj sob lus xwb. "Hla dej yuav hle khau" yog
ib sob paj lug uas txhais hais tias "tsiv teb tsaws chaw yuav hle hau"
no xwb. Yog yus ho muab xav hais tias ob sob lus no puav leej yog paj
lug ces nws yauv txhais nws lawm ib yam lawm. Li phoojywg Lajntxiag
hais uas sob lus "tsiv teb tsaws chaw yuav hle hau" no nws comparable
tau rau sob lus "txog luaj ntuj yoog luag txuj" no. Txhais tau hais
tias nws twb hais lub ntsiab lawm, tsis yog paj lug uas tseem yuav tau
txhais. Tsuas yog hais tias ob sob lus no yog cov sob lus muaj kuab
xwb (lus laus neeg siv).
Yog nej saib tej movie hais txog kev ua nom ua tswv yav thaum ub, nej
yuav pom hais tias, txhua txhua lub meejmom (rank) nyias muaj nyias ib
lub crown los cim. Kuv ntseeg hais tias lo lus "hau" yog lub "crown"
ntawd xwb. Yog li ces hle hau kuj yog hle crown xwb. Thaum yus hle hau
lawm ces yus tsuas zoo li ib tug pej xeem thiab xwb.
