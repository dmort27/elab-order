you have to look at the bigger picture of "tsa hauv toj".  originally,
the purpose of the event is to "reorganize" the mong who were
separated (ib tus khiav qab roob, ib tus khiav qab ha), probably for
the purpose of "reorganize" a counterattack and to "regroup" by
allowing the flag of txiv yawg to be shown up high in the mountain so
that moob thiaj li pom, thiaj li pauv rov los nrhiav, los ua ib pab ib
pawg.  however,  tom qab no ces...moob lub tswv yim txawv lawm....as
part of the event (because there is no more war), hmoob pib
"gathering" around, dancing, singing, playing qeej around tus ncej
ntxhoo, along with sports and fun (such as ntaus tis qaib li koj
hais).  in addition, the "pob pov" part was introduced..and kwvtxhiaj
(and all other music and games and events) were introduced to make it
"fun."
