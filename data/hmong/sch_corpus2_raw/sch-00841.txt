there is a difference between "slang" and your typical "euphemism" and "pajlus".
slang:  muab qhov zoo coj los hais phem
euphemism:  muab qhov phem coj los hais zoo
puas yog li no ne?  vim kuv mloog mas zoo li feem coob muab xav tias pajlus yog slang sub na...
example of euphemism:  koj niam koj txiv noj dabtsi yug koj es yuav yug tau koj zoo nkauj ua luaj = your fat!
example of pajlus: hais lus taug ceg fajkhaum ntau ces mag kuas nceb, yus tsi paub los ceg ntoo qhuav raug yus.  thaum no ces yus yuav liab ntsuab liab ntsuab....
slang:  zoo nkauj tuag!  = drop dead gorgeous = zoo nkauj tiag tiag...
what i'm interested in is...seb nej cov laus zog puas txhais tau tias pajlus yog dabtsi tiag tiag...es xob muab pajlus los xyaws "slang."
