Qhov peb hmoob sib koom khob haus cawv mas yog the primary lawm ntag
nawb. Kuv li twb tau 10 tawm xyoo no kuv yeej tsi lam koom nrog leej
twg hauv khob ua ke li lawm laiv. Mus koom noj mov los kuv yeej hauv
kuv li xwb, lawv lub los txog cais hla xwb. Yog leej twg ua nyaum tsi
kam hla cais kuv yeej tsi thab nrog nws noj mov li thiab no laiv. Yus
txoj sia tseem ceeb tshaj pluag niag mov noj ntawm. Qhov tsi mus ntsib
doctor los kuj yuav yog me me thiab tabsis tsi loj.
