hab tsi taag le hov xwb, nwg tub puamxeeb yog ib tug mivnyuas tub
dlagnwg yeem yuav muaj peevxwm nrhav kev kuam tugkheej tsi txhob

yaav taag lug lawm, puab puas tau ua dlaabtsi pauj koj rov qaab le?
Koj tsi paub khib le hab lov?
Caag es yug txhaj yuav ca kuam tugkheej txuj kev xaav cale laam tau
laam xaav dlaabtsi le nuav?  Tsum kag!  Tsi yoom xaav dlaabtsi le nuav
ntxiv lawm.  Txawm puab yuav muab tsi tau ib yaam cuabyeej dlaabtsi ua
kev zoo sab rua yug; txawm puab yuav tsi tau has lu nyuas lug uatsaug
rua yug xwb; hab txawm puab yuav tsi tau ua ntau tsaav ntau yaam kws
puab ntxim ua rua yug los kaavlam.  Ncu ntsoov tas puab muaj cov
ntshaav kws zoo ibyaam le yug cov ntshaav hab.  Txawm puab yuav has
tawm tsi tau nrug lub qhovncauj los thaum yug saib puab cov qhovmuag
xwb ces yug tub yeem paub zoo lawm tas puab lub sab xaav  tau yaam
dlaabtsi lawm.
Yug yog tug hlubpuab yog cov yau.  Puab tsi tau xaiv kuam puab yug
lug ua tug yau yug.  Vim le caag yug txhaj yuav xaam nuj xaam nqe rua
puab?  Yug yog tug hlub.  Yug teg dlejnum yog saibxyuas cov yau.
Txawm yug yuav tau txais kev txomnyem npaum le caag los tsuav kuam cov
yau tsi txhob tau ntsib le yug xwb ces ntxim yog qhov txaus sab rua
yug lawm.  Puab ntxim tau lub neej kws tsi txhob paub txug txuj kev
nyuab sab le yug.  Txawm yug yuav yog tug raug xaiv kuam tau txais kev
lwj sab npaum le caag los kaavlam, tsuav yog  yug paab tau puab kuam
muaj lub caij tau moog kawm txujci es sub txhajle yuav paub muaj kev
vaam kuam luj tshaaj qub tuaj xwb ces yeem txaus rua yug lawm.  Tsuav
yug tau ua txuj phuam puag puab lub ntuj uake xwb ces tau lawm.
