dag hais tias yuav mus hais un qhib kev rau no hmoob thiaj los tas zog.
saib zaum no un puas pab. hnov hais tias hmoob mus nrog un tham tim new
york coob kawg lawm no. sim cia saib zaum no hmoob yuav los qw li
cas.zoo li hnov hais tias xyoo no muaj ob peb pab hmoob mus txog tim un
lawm no laiv cov yawg. ntshe yuav tau noj tiag paub. raws li hnov hais
ces yog kum xiong(lao human rights), laura xiong(hmong human rights),
zongkhang yang(long walk for freedom), thiab tseem muaj ib pab hu ua
hmong world congress(xeng xiong),william yang (hmong languistic
institute) thiab no tiag. cov hmoob no paus sib koom los nyias tseem
mus hais nyias zaj xwb. nej leej twg paub qhov tseeb no sim qhia rau
peb sob.yog coob pab npaum no mas kuv ntshai nyias yuav rov los hais
nyias zaj kom hmoob rov tsis paub ntseeg tus twg lau.  tejzaum pob zeb
tuag lawm cia saib leej twg yuav qw hais tias un twb nrog daws npas ham
lawm no. hmoob coob npaum no yog tseem hais tias lawv mus hais tau un
qhib kev lawm thiab no mas ntshe cov hmoob nyob nplog teb yuav sib tw
khiav los rau thaib teb zaum no. yog nej leej twg hnov cov hmoob no tus
twg tshaj tawm xov tooj cua los yog hais tawm koom paj xoos no qhia peb
paub.
