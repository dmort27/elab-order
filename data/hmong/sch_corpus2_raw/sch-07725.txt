TXOJ XOVXWM MOOZOO: LEEJTWG YOG TUS CUAVTSHEEJ?
Published Friday, August 01, 2003
TXOJ XOVXWM MOOZOO * LEEJTWG YOG TUS CUAVTSHEEJ?
WHO IS A CULT?
*
I Yohanes 4:1-6, COV hlub, tsistxhob ntseeg txhua tus plig, tiamsis sim
cov plig seb lawv puasyog Vajtswv tug: vimtias muaj coob cov cuav saub
tau tawm mus thoob ntiajteb. Nej paub Vajtswv tus Plig rawsli qhov nov:
Txhua tus plig leesntsoov ntawh Yexus Khrlixtos yog los hauv lub cev
yog Vajtswv tug: Thiab txhua tus plig ntawh tsis leesntsoov ntawh Yexus
Khrlixtos yog tau los hauv lub cev tsis yog Vajtswv tug: ces nov yog
ntawh tus plig tawmtsam-khrlixtos, tus nej tau-hnov ntawh yuav los; ces
nimno twb nyob hauv ntiajteb lawm. Nej yog Vajtswv tug, cov me minyuam,
thiab tau-yeej lawv lawm: vimtias loj tshaj yog Nws tus nyobhauv nej,
tshaj nws tus nyobhauv ntiajteb. Lawv yog ntiajteb tug: yoglintawh lawv
tham yam yog ntiajteb tug, thiab ntiajteb hnovntsoov lawv. Peb yog
Vajtswv tug: nws tus paubntsoov Vajtswv hnovntsoov peb; nws tus tsis
yog Vajtswv tug tsis hnovntsoov peb. Rawsli qhov nov peb paub tus plig
ntawm kev tseeb, thiab tus plig ntawm kev tshuamyuaj.
