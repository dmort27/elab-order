*** Here are a few more phrases, with the PHONEME (N-R-H) in the Hmong
language:
1.  NRHAB koj ob sab ceg
2.  Mus NRHIAV tus neeg poob zoo
3.  Nws mus kev NRHAWV NRHIS
("nrawv nroov"/"nrawm nroos" is also another term)
3.  Tus hluas nkauj rau NRHOOB
4.  "Hais lus NRHIAS RIAM" (left Laos when I was 7 and never did
develop my Hmong beyond 2nd grade level, so I've never heard of
such a phraseology; though it seems sound enough.)
5.  Teb chaws NRHAWV
6.  Tsob ntoo NRHEEB (nrheeb)
7.  Peb mus xyuas tiaj NRHA tuam tshoj los
8.  Tsis txhob hais lus NRHOO (nrhoov nrhoov)
9.  "Nyug ntse muag e! Cas koj yuav NRHEEB ua luaj, na!"
10. Lub hau tsi rov NRHAW (nthaw/hlav)

Ib tsoom phooj ywg,
Peb yeej muaj lo lus "nrhais" li Tzexa hais los ntawd. (nrhais nriav =
nrhais quas nrav). Hais lus nrhais nriav haus lub sablawj (cassete
player) tuaj.
Los lus "nrha" los peb yeej ib txwm siv thiab. (nrha = ntshaa). Mus
kev tus niag tes taw "nrha" ris li niag vaub kib thiab niag roob ris.
Kuv los kuj xav li ib txhia phooj ywg hais thiab, los lus "nrhais" no
tsis muaj ntsiab lug ze rau lo lus "spelling". Yog vim li cas peb ho
tsis lam siv lo uas peb twb ib txwm siv lawm xwb. "TXIS" los sis "TXIS
NTAWV".
