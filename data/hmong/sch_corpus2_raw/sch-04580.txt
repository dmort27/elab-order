Dear Friends and SCHlers,
It was my privilege to have the opportunity to meet with the
representatives of CARE Rak Thai, Integral Trust Fund and Doctors
Without Borders and the Third Army Colonels in Huay Nam Khao in the
past week to discuss about the Bamboo Housing Project for Hmong Dej
Dawb. The budget for the Bamboo Housing Project is 2 million baht
which will cover the Bamboo and Thatch (xyoob and cuam nqeeb). We
agreed to work collaboratively with CARE Rak Thai and Integral Trust
Fund to raise the 2 million baht for the project. CARE Rak Thai
already came up with their contribution to cover 900 houses and Hmong
America will raise the money to pay for 600 more houses.
Cov xyoob thiab nqeeb uas coj los ua cov tsev, tseem yog luab xwb, vim
yog CARE Rak Thai tsuas pab lawv tug xwb, tshuav Hmoob America tug.
Peb koom haum HIHRW ua tsaug rau cov thawj coj Hmoob nyob St Paul, ua
tom qab kuv mus ntsib cov NGOs thiab the Colonels tim Thaib teb rov
los txog, lawv txaus siab take the responsibility to raise the money
as request by the Colonels and CARE Rak Thai. Qhov no yog peb cov
Hmoob txoj kev koom tes tau nrog luag lwm haiv neeg kev pab peb tsev
neeg Hmoob.
Ib tsoom phoojywg, yog nej muaj lub siab hlub txog pebcov kwv tij
Hmoob, thov sponsor cov tsev nqeeb uas lawv ua rau cov Kwv tij Hmoob
Dej Dawb mus nyob. Ib lub tsev yog raug 1,500 baht los yog $44. Txawm
nej yuav sponsor tau ib lub los yog 10 lub, nws yog nej lub txiaj
ntsim pab peb cov Hmoob Dej Dawb kom lawv tau lub tsev nyob.
Kev pab zaum no, yog pab cov Nom Tswv Thaib thiab cov NGOs ua tsev rau
peb cov Hmoob nyob. Txawm hais tias Hmoob Dej Dawb yuav mus nyob 10
hnub los yog 10 xyoo, peb tsis hais, tsuav peb pab ua tau cov tsev rau
peb cov kwvtij Hmoob mus nyob nkaum nag ua ntej tso. vim yuav txog lub
caij ntuj nag, nom tswv Thaib thiaj yuav ua kom tau cov tsev rau lawv
mus nyob ua ntej lub 6 hli ntuj nag los.
Tamsim no, lawv twb ua tau 1200 tawm lub tsev lawm, tabsi tsuas yog
tsa tau, thiab vov tau ib co xwb, tseem tshuav ntau lub tsis tau vov.
Tsis tau muaj cov tsev ua xov tau li, vim xyoob tsis txaus. April 24,
lawv thauj tau 20,000 cuam nqeeb tuaj ntxiv. Lawv tseem yuav mus
nrhiav saib puas muaj neeg muag xyoob, yuav txiav coj los xov cov tsev
no.
Cov Tub Rog Thaib cia siab ntsoov tias Hmoob America yuav raise tau
cov nyiaj mus pab lawv them cov xyoob thiab nqeeb.
Yog nej leejtwg xav paub txog lub Bamboo Housing Project no, thov hu
rau the US Base-Project Manager, Txoos Xob Lauj, los yog Pabtha thiab
loo Patha koom Haum Hmoob 18 Xeem, Neng Vang Lo thiab Solomon Kong
lawv. Txhua yam haujlwm, muab cob tag rau lawv lawm. Tsis ntev no,
lawv yuav open ib lub trust fund account rau nej xa nyiaj mus rau. Cov
nyiaj yuav muab transfer directly mus rau CARE Rak Thai, lawv mam
reimburse mus them cov Housing Materials.
Yog nej leejtwg muaj lus nug txog qhov kuv mus ntsib cov
representatives of the CARE Rak Thai, Doctors Without Border, Integral
Trust Fund thiab the Third Region Army Colonels, please don't hesitate
to post your questions here or email me at ***@yahoo.com.
Thank you for taking the time to read this posting.
