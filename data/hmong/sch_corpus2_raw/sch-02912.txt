Thaus quaspuj hab txivneej muaj teebmeem nyob rua sau yaajceeb:
1.  Nwg tug ntsuj huv yeebceeb chim heev.  Tug ntsuj ntawd yog tug
zaaj kwv nphau dlej hab nphau aav lug tsim kev kubntxhuv.  Tug zaaj
huv yeebceeb heev dlhau ces nwg tswj tsi tau.
2. Thaus nwg tug zaaj huv yeebceeb tswj nwg lawm, nwg txhajle tua nwg
tug quaspuj sau yeebceeb nuav tuag.
3. Tug quaspuj los zoo tuabyaam, nwg ca nwg tug ntshis huv yeebceeb
tswj nwg.  Tug ntshis huv yeebceeb xaav nyob rua tojzeb hab tsuas sab.
4. Thaus tug tshis huv yeebceeb tswj nwg lawm, nwg txhajle tsiv tawm
ntawm nwg tug quasyawg moog rua tug tshab saibseb lub neej puas yuav
kaaj.
5. Thaus zaaj nqog tshis nyob rua huv yeebceeb, tshis kub dlaig zaaj
caajdlaab.
6. Nyob rua sau yeebceeb, tug quasyawg tua tug quaspuj, huas quaspuj
txujkev tuag los ua rua nwg tuag tuabyaam nkaus.
