Puag xyoo 1986 los mas txhua txhua xyoo kuv yeej hnob peb yawg
Vajhmoob hais tias lwm xyoo mas peb yuav tau mus noj peb caug
nplogteb.  Niaj hnub no peb yeej tau mus noj peb caug nplogteb laqwm
tiag.  Peb yuav tau ua yawg tsaug thiab mloog yawg hais rau qhov yog
tsi muaj yawg mas peb yeej tsi tau mus noj peb caug nplogteb ib zaug
li ib yam li yog tsi muaj yawg mas peb yeej tsi tau tuaj rau America
lossis French, Australia thiab ntau ntau lub tebchaws.
Yawg yeej yog muaj peev xwm kawg nkaus lawm tiag.  Peb txhua tus yuav
tau mloog yawg hais peb thiab li zoo.  Nim no yawg tsi hais li mas cov
tim DD thiaj pheej raug teeb meem thiab mag xa cov qab.  Kuv xav tias
muaj ib hnub yawg yuav sawv los hais mas nyiaj tus puav kuj yuav tau
tawm mus txawv tebchaws thiab.
