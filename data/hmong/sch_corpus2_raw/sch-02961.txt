Thaum tuaj tis-Tshoob/kug, "TUAJ TXUJKEV NQAIM ES YUAV ROV TXUJKEV
DLAAV, YOG TUAJ TIS-NEEJ TIS-TSAAV." Yog lug ntxeev... thaum tuaj mas
tuaj txuj kev dlaav, tabsis thaum rov ces tau txuj kev nqaim
heev...tseem maag tshev, maag nyos, nyajtxag los poob taag. Rov tuab
cov chim chim lawm xwb.
Uantej kws luas yuav muab tshoob, luas muab khi kuam khov khov...luas
nug tas, " MEJ TUAJ, MEJ YUAV PEM NEEGEEB LOS PEM NEEGTSAAV?" es NAM/
TXIV TOM UB QHOVROOJ QHEB IBTXHIB LOS OBTXHIB?"
Yeej laavntuj/teb tas nam/txiv tsev-Vuag tsizoo sim zeb, tsevnqeeb
tsizoo sim tawg...nyam hab yuav tag txhajle tuaj.
Thaum khi tau lawm, maamle has kevraug kevtxhum...muab 120 txuj cais
lug nthuav has...30 xyoo tom ntej ub tej teebmeem. Has 10 zaaj, nplua
10 pobnyaj..dlha-peg dlha-nraag saablaaj...

le-ub, lenua taag maamle muab tshoob.

tshuav nua, 120 txuj kev nplua.
Tau kag nyaab ces sibntxuv, sib-ua-plaub txug ncua nub kws tuagkag los
plaub tseem has tsitaag.
Cov tubntsuag, yeej tsimuaj $7000 moog yuav quaspuj.
Tsimuaj kwv/tij lossis phoojywg, tsimuaj tug moog paab ua dlejnum.
Yog nam/txiv tsaayim, es them nqe nyaab, Tub/nyaab nyob nam/txiv nqe
taag tam. Yuavtau koom, teev/tam nam/txiv...tuag los yuavtau tua nyuj
ntaunpua hab ntaa lub nteeg.
Moob sibntxub sibntxaug, ibtug sawv nom, cov twb ntsis-phom rua. Thaum
txug tshaav rog, moob rov taw ntsis-phom rua moob.

caum tua, tuag tsheej pawg.

xeem...ibtxheej dlhau ibtxheej, ibtam dlhau ibtam.

pumdleb.

tradition rua????????
Koj puas sau nyaj???????
Caag koj tsihlub ibtug moob le??????
Koj ho hlub lwmtug mev, dlub, lossis Asmiskas dlua??????

khwv pab koj tug txiv them koj le nqe taubhau.
