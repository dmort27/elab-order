rog rog rev, qig qig taub, do do hau thiab dub dub tawv los vim yog koj

phem phem rau peb cov Hmoob nyob rau hauv no los vim yog koj yog Nplog
tiag nev.  Ua cas thaum ub koj niam tsis muaj me nyuam ho tsis mus deev
Hmoob es tseem ho mus deev Nplog xya tau koj thiab nas niag ntsej muag
Nplog ruam.  Tus nyuag Nplog ruam koj es tseem yuav tuaj cem kuv tus
niam laus mob ua luaj li av.  Yog txij hnub no mus lawm tod koj tsis
tsum, tseem tuaj hais lus phem ntxiv rau peb cov Hmoob thiab, kuv yuav
muab koj lub npe Hmoob tseem qhia kiag rau sawvdaws nyob hauv no paub
ntag nawb.  Qhia rau koj paub li ntawd xwb os niag ntsej muag Nplog
ruam rog rev taub haus loj am lawg.
