Kuv lam hais me me li qhov kuv paub nawb.
Dab nyuj = Cow monster
Dab npua = Pig monster
Nyuj dab = Cow sacrify (offer to ancester only if is asked for thru'
dream or shaman)
Npua dab roog / Npua tai = Pig sacrify (offer to ancestor only if asked
for thru' dream or shaman)
Npua dab = Pig sacrify (offer to ancester during wedding)
Qaib dab = Chicken sacrify (offer to ancester during wedding)
Dab qhuas = religion: dab = spiritual guest, qhua = human being guest
Dab tshos = not sure but could be a simbal or script for sub-religion.
see these notes:
1. Lub dab tshow muaj 9 txwg thiab 3 ntsau qhia tau hais tias tsev neeg
no yog dab qhuas 9 txig peb ntsau. (txwg = txig)
2. Thaum tsis nco qab yus dab qhuas lawm ces mus nug muam phauj vim
muam phauj yog tus tau lub tsho laus uas muaj daim paj ntaub dab tsho
no.
3. Peb hu lub dab tshos no ua "paj NTAUB dab tshos", thiab peb siv los
lus hais tias txawj NTAUB txawj ntawv. Tus neeg txawj NTAUB yog tus uas
txawj nyeem daim dab tsho no.
