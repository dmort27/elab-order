Yog peb Hmoob pheej ua le lub sab xaav xwb mas tuab zoo tsaam peb tsi muaj chaws tsiv qaum .
Peb yuav tau lug leeg txim rua Vaajtswv .
Kuv xaav tas Vaajtswv coj peb tuaj rua lub tebchaws nuav kuam peb txawj leeg peb tej kev txhum hab kuam peb lug ntseeg Vaajtswv .
Tabsis yog peb tsi ntseeg Vaajtswv mas peb yuav tsi tau koob moov hab peb yuav raug Vaajtswv foom tsi zoo .
Peb yuav taug txuj kev kws ( Yelemis 15 : 6 - 7 ) has tas .
" Tug Tswv has tas , mej tau tso kuv tseg lawm , mej tig nraub qaum rua kuv .
Vim le ntawm kuv txhaj ntaus mej , rua qhov kuv tswj tsi tau kuv txuj kev chim lawm .
Kuv yuav muab mej ntab tawm ntawm txhua lub nroog huv lub tebchaws nuav moog ib yaam le cua ntsawj tej quav nyaab .
Kuv yuav ua rua mej kws yog kuv tsaav tuabneeg kuam puam tsuaj , kuv tua mej tej menyuam vim mej tsi tso mej tej kev txhum cheryang koj qhob nuav puas yog tas mekas yog vaajtswv nua maj ?
yog tas mekas yog vaajtswv nua nwg yuav tsum pum peb cov hmoob tuag coob tim nplog teb lawm hab os nwg yuav tau moog paab tag vim peb hmoob tsi dlaag mekas ne , hab vim le caag es tug vaajtswv nuav yuav tuaj dlaag peb ua dlev caum kauv rua nwg lub sij kws nws xaav kuam pebhmoob paab nwg xwb mas , es dlau le ntawd lawm ces nws ho tsiv rov qaab lawm es nwg ho tso peb ib txha hmoob tseg lawm ces kuj tsi fair lawm hab mas .
