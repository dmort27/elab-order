Dhau ntawv lawm , lawv tsis refer hais tias lawv yog sivyis lawm .
Qhov lawv hais li no tejzaum yog li PY YTK hais lawm , lawv hais tias lawv yog Sivyis no kom dab ntshai xwb .
Yog koj ua twb zoo mloog lawv ua neeb , koj yuav hnov hais tias lawv siv lo lus " sivyis " no los tam lo lus " kuv " .
Example : Thaum ze ze lawv yuav nqis neeb , lawv hais li no : Sivyis hnubnyoog tuaj lawm ntev , sivyis yuav rawm rais fib tse ; sivyis hnubnyoog tuaj lawm ntxov , sivyis yuav rais rov .
