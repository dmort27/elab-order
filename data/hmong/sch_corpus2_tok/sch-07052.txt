Hnub i tau nyeem ntawv xovxwm hauv The Nation hais tias muaj li 600 - 700 leej Hmoob nyob Xeem Khuam thiab Xavnyajnpulis tau tawm tom hav zoov los lawm vim hais tias LPDR tau muab kev zam txim rau lawv ( amnesty ) lawm .
Ho tsis paub tseeb hais tias cov tawm los no puas muaj txog coob li ntawd thiab puas muaj cov Cob Fab ( Vaj Tswv Cim Vwj lawv pab ) nrog .
Tam li kuv paub Muas Tuam Thawj tsis yog Cob Fab , nws yog cov Pab Tib Kas .
Hnov li lus cua mas peb cov Hmoob nyob saum roob muaj coob txog 6 pab diam tabsis lawv tsis yog Cob Fab tas tus .
Ib co nrog Paj Kaub thiab Zoov Zuag lawv cov Cob Fab , ib co nrog VP , ib co tseem nrog luag lwm leej lwm tus qub nom tswv Hmoob .
Tamsim no mas tseem tsis tau paub hais tias ua cov tawm los puas yog cov Cob Fab .
Tam kuv xav mas cov Cob Fab yuav tsis kam tawm los thiab yuav yog ib co uas LPDR ntxias nyuaj tshaj plaws vim lawv muaj lub zeem muag loj heev .
Cov yuav xub tawm mas yuav yog cov Pab Tib Kas .
